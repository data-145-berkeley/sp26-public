## Exam Policies

- **Closed book**: No notes, books, or electronic devices
- **No calculators**: Problems are designed to not require one
- **Conflicts**: Notify the instructor within the first two weeks if you have
  a scheduling conflict

## How to Prepare

1. Review all lecture materials and problem sets
2. Work through practice exams under timed conditions
3. Attend the review session (date TBD)
4. Come to office hours with questions
